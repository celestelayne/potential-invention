ARCH A4988 Webring Directory

If you've reached this page, you've probably already visited one of the websites on the ARCH A4988 Webring. Welcome! This webring contains the homepages for students in the ARCH A4988 course, Personal Webcraft: Digital Gardens and the Future of Individuality. This course is inspired by the work of Olia Lialina and J.R. Carpenter.

What is the small web?

The small web is made up of people creating their own little corners of the internet—personal sites that reflect their quirks, interests, and creativity. It’s less about polished templates and more about experimenting, playing, and expressing yourself in unique ways.
